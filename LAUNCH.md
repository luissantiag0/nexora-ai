## 1. Product Hunt Preparation

### 1.1 Tagline

### 1.2 Short description
AverionAI combines CRM, document generation, invoicing, and AI automation into a single SSR-secured SaaS platform. Built for teams that need intelligent lead management without the complexity of enterprise tools.

### 1.3 Long description
AverionAI is a modern SaaS platform that brings together CRM, AI-powered document generation, automated invoicing, and business analytics in one secure environment.

Unlike traditional CRMs that require weeks of setup and multiple integrations, AverionAI works immediately. Create leads, classify them with AI, generate professional invoices and pitch decks, and track your pipeline — all from a single interface rendered server-side for security and performance.

Built with Astro SSR, Prisma, and Stripe, AverionAI delivers enterprise-grade security without enterprise complexity. Every page is server-rendered. Sessions are protected. Data stays encrypted.

- CRM with AI-powered lead classification and pipeline management

### 1.6 FAQ
**What makes AverionAI different from other CRMs?**
AverionAI is built from the ground up with AI at the core, not as an add-on. Document generation, lead classification, and content creation are native features, not third-party integrations.

**Is my data secure?**
Yes. AverionAI uses SSR architecture, encrypted sessions, CSRF protection, rate limiting, and comprehensive audit logging. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

**Can I try before buying?**
Yes. We offer a 30-minute free trial with full access to all premium features. No credit card required.

**What happens when my trial ends?**
Your account remains active. You lose access to premium features until you subscribe. Your data is never deleted.
